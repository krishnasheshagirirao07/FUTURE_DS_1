# Business Sales Performance Analytics 📊

## Objective
To analyze historical sales data for a retail Superstore to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Tools Used
* **Python** (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) - For Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* **Power BI** - For Interactive Dashboard Creation
* **Jupyter Notebook**

## Key Business Insights
1. **Sales Trends:** Revenue shows strong seasonality, peaking consistently in Q4 (November/December) for holiday shopping, followed by sharp drops in Q1.
2. **Category Performance:** **Technology** is the highest-grossing category, significantly outperforming Furniture and Office Supplies.
3. **Top Product:** The *Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier* is the single most valuable product, generating over $61,000 in revenue alone.
4. **Regional Dominance:** The **West** region leads the company in total sales ($725K+), closely followed by the East. 

## Recommendations
* Increase marketing spend in the West and East regions to capitalize on high-performing markets.
* Prepare inventory and supply chain operations specifically for the Q4 holiday surge.
* Create bundled promotions around the high-value Technology products to drive average order value.
